Manali

Manali, a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, is known for its scenic beauty and outdoor adventures.Famous for its adventure sports, Solang Valley offers paragliding, skiing, zorbing, and snowboarding during the winter. In summer, the valley becomes a lush green hub for trekking and camping.

Goa

Goa, a small state on the western coast of India, is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and historical significance.Goa is renowned for its pristine beaches, offering something for every type of traveler.

Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, is a breathtaking destination known for its natural beauty, rich culture, and diverse landscapes.The capital city of Sikkim, Gangtok is a vibrant hill station with stunning views of Mount Kanchenjunga. It offers a mix of urban attractions, monasteries, and trekking opportunities, making it a great base for exploring the state.

Kasaul

Kasauli, a serene hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, is known for its natural beauty, colonial-era charm, and peaceful environment.Established in 1880, the Kasauli Club is one of the oldest clubs in India and still retains its old-world charm. Though entry is restricted to members, the building and its surroundings provide a glimpse into British-era social life.
